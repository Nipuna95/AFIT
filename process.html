Process

We believe that all good work need proper resourcing. At AFIT Connects, we allocate resources for each task in a very well organized manner. Our staff with great caliber and industry knowledge will take care of your assignment and ensure that sophisticated technology is used to improve the optimal quality and effectiveness of your work.

Information security is a top priority in all our business processes and we use latest technology and stringent processes to ensure that the security of our clients' data is highly secured.

For each task completed, ABC will assign an independent team to ensure the quality of the work performed by each staff member and that information is handled in line with information security guidelines.

We do not cut corners, we believe in perfect execution of each work assignment. For this, our work review processes have been designed to ensure error free completion of all deliverables which will go through three reviews as a minimum standard. To this end, there ware three reviews The review process consists of a peer-review, a team leader review and an independent quality review.

We always value learning, education and consistent improvement. Our L&E process have been designed to provide our staff members with essential training periodically. This process ensures that our staff keep abreast with the latest industry knowledge and skillset.
