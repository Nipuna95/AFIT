Why Sri Lanka?

Sri Lanka is fast becoming a regional hub for Financial and Accounting Outsourcing (FAO) services. With all national universities offering finance and accounting degrees of global standards, Sri Lanka produces many professionals in the field every year. In the sphere of professional accountancy education the main accountancy bodies - ACCA, CA, CIMA, AAT & CMA- facilitate enormous learning opportunities for aspiring professionals with many private universities also producing exceptional talent. Although the level of expertise and professionalism is in par with the global standards, the professional fees is not as expensive compared to most other countries given the low cost of living in Sri Lanka. Additionally, Sri Lanka has also made significant progress in recent years as a technology hub in Asia making high speed internet and latest technological advancements widely accessible. All in all, Sri Lanka has gained strategic advantage for offering outsourcing back end office services particularly in the field of finance and accounting.
