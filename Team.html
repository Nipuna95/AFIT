Our Team

Our staff are passionate about what they do - they are enthusiastic, hardworking and unfailingly exceed clients' expectations. Our staff are the single most reason of our success, hence we take utmost commitment to taking good care of them.

Our processes have been designed to enable effective work-life balance for staff while introducing measures for upskilling our workforce for greater efficiency at delivering exceptional service. We take continuous and consistent efforts to ensure that our human resource is a cut above the rest in finance industry.

For this, we make sure that:

Our staff are highly skilled in the field of finance

Only graduated and qualified accountants are recruited

Staff members are registered in relevant professional field including Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka (CA), Certified Management Accountants (CMA), and Association of Accounting Technicians (AAT).

Staff are regularly trained to keep abreast with the changes in global reporting, compliance and taxation updates

And are trained to make the optimal use of latest technological developments

We are a small team of twenty chartered accountants, accountancy graduates and members of professional accounting bodies who can take care of your most complex and demanding finance assignments with exceptional caliber.

Dushan Thumbovila

CA, BSc (Finance) Special

CEO

Dushan Thumbovila is a Chartered Accountant who holds the membership of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka. Dushan also has obtained a finance special degree from the University of Sri Jayewardenepura of Sri Lanka. With more than 10 years of experience in the accountancy and finance sector, Dushan's career has already seen peaks as a co-founder of AFIT Connects and the CEO of AFIT Connects. Dushan has also held senior positions at PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC) from 2010 to 2013 before setting up his own venture of outsources accounting and finance firm. Parallel to all the he does, Dushan also holds the position of Managing Partner of a Sri Lankan Audit Firm.

Nuwan Srimal

CA, BSc (Accountancy) Special

Head of Operations

Nuwan Srimal is a Chartered Accountant who holds the membership of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka. Added to his academic qualifications, Nuwan also holds an accountancy special degree from the University of Sri Jayewardenepura of Sri Lanka. He started off his career as an accountant with Deloitte Colombo office and has so far accumulated more than ten years of experience in the field of accountancy and finance. Nuwan is a core member of the AFIT Connects leadership team serving as the Director of Operations. Before joining AFIT Connects, Nuwan has served in various managerial and senior positions in Sri Lankan companies including Swiss Lloyds and Deloites.
