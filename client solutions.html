Our Solutions

Our client surveys testify that the businesses are making a massive saving of 75% on their costs of handing back office finance and accounting work since we started taking care of this work for them. What is more, this has enabled the businesses to focus on other core functions of their work thereby enhancing the overall gains and efficiency of their operations. We are happy to offer you a free trial period before joining with us, so you can experience the difference with absolutely no commitment. If pleased with our services, you can sign up for a plan that works best for you.

Part-time Engagements

If your firm requires only limited hours of works per week or month, we can allocate a team member to work as per your requirement. E.g. If the need is a staff member for 10 hours per week or 20 hours per week, we can allocate you a staff member for the specified timeframe.

Project Based Engagements

If your requirement is project based, we can work on a solution that suits you. We will agree on the hours of service required for each project and customize the engagement accordingly.

Quality Review

Even though you will be allocated with staff members, we will ensure the quality and the timeliness of work as team leaders and quality assurance team will always check the deliverables and ensure that all tasks are in the correct standard and completed within agreed timeframes.

Communication

In addition to the regular the email exchanges and other online communication channels, we also use a CRM tool (Podio, Trollo etc.) to keep up job-wise communication. The team allocated to your accounting firm and your in-house staff members can communicate with each other on a project basis via the CRM tools. Furthermore, every client will be allocated with a team leader who will manage all jobs of an individual accounting firm and will always be responsible for communicate with the accounting firm
